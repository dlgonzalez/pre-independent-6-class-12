📘 Grammar Reinforcement (Unit 11)

You reviewed the rules of preference in the previous class, but this grammar requires repetition. Let's briefly recap the formulas and take the 15-Item Challenge to master the tricky prepositions!

The Preference Formulas Recap

1. Prefer (General Facts)

prefer + [V-ing / Noun] + to + [V-ing / Noun]
  • • I prefer donating to volunteering.

2. Prefer (Infinitive)

prefer + to [Verb] + rather than + [Base Verb]
  • • I prefer to help rather than watch.

3. Would Rather (Specific)

would rather + [Base Verb] + than + [Base Verb]

NO "to" allowed!

  • • I'd rather give money than give clothes.

4. Would Prefer (Specific)

would prefer + to [Verb]
  • • I would prefer to focus on the earthquake victims.

Operation Rescue: Venezuela

Urgent Crisis Response Negotiation

🚨 The Scenario

Last week, a massive earthquake struck several regions in Venezuela, causing significant damage to infrastructure, hospitals, and homes. Thousands of people are displaced.

You and your partner are directors of an International Relief NGO. You have received an emergency budget of $500,000. You cannot fund everything. You must debate and choose how to allocate the funds.

Sector Option A ($250k) Option B ($250k)
Health Send mobile surgical teams Ship 10 tons of medicine
Shelter Build heavy-duty tent camps Repair damaged apartments
Food Airdrop ready-to-eat meals Fund local community kitchens

How to Execute This Mission

⏱️ Step 1: Solo Strategy (3 mins)

Review the matrix. Choose exactly TWO options you want to fund (Total $500k). You must leave one sector completely unfunded for now. Write down your reasons.

🗣️ Step 2: The Negotiation (10 mins)

Pair up with your co-director. You must agree on the final 2 choices. You must use preference grammar to argue your points.

Use these phrases to argue:

"I would rather fund mobile surgical teams than ship medicine, because..."

"I prefer building tent camps to repairing apartments right now, because..."

"I would prefer to fund local kitchens, but I understand your point."
🎤 Step 3: Press Conference (5 mins)

Stand up and announce your team's final decision to the class. Explain why you preferred those specific options over the alternatives.
